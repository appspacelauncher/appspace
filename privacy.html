Privacy Policy for AppSpace Launcher

Last updated: August 6, 2025

Introduction

AppSpace Launcher ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application.

Data Collection and Storage

What We Don't Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your data on external servers
  • No Network Monitoring: We do not monitor your network activity

What We Store Locally

All data is stored locally on your Mac and never leaves your device:

  • App Preferences: Your settings, pinned apps, folders, and customizations
  • App Icons: Any custom icons you choose for apps or folders
  • Pinned Locations: Bookmarks to folders you've pinned (stored locally)
  • Password Protection: Any master password you set (stored securely using macOS keychain)

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • StoreKit: For subscription management (handled entirely by Apple)
  • macOS Keychain: For secure password storage (Apple's built-in security feature)

No Other Third-Party Services

We do not use any analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party data collection tools.

Data Security

Local Storage

  • All your data is stored locally on your Mac
  • We use Apple's recommended security practices
  • Passwords are stored using macOS Keychain encryption

Network Access

  • The app only makes network connections for:
    • App Store subscription verification (via StoreKit)
    • Opening network locations you choose to access
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal information to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Port to another service

Your data is entirely under your control on your local device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, this is not applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: Email Us!
  • GitHub: https://github.com/appspacelauncher/appspace

Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy law requirements don't apply to our app, but we're committed to transparency about our practices.

AppSpace Launcher is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device, where it belongs.
